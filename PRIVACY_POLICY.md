# Privacy Policy for Agriገበያ (EtFruit)

Last updated: July 07, 2026

At Agriገበያ (EtFruit), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Data
While using our app, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including:
- **Name**: To identify you on the platform and to other buyers/sellers.
- **Phone Number**: Used for OTP authentication and verification.
- **Location Information (Region, Zone, Woreda)**: To facilitate agricultural trades and matching local buyers with local sellers.

### Device and Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information when you access the app, including:
- **Device details**: Device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage details**: Logs of actions you perform, error/crash reports, and general engagement analytics.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:
- **Authentication & Security**: To verify your account via OTP and prevent fraud.
- **Order Processing & Facilitation**: To display merchant location details, process agricultural orders, and support messaging.
- **Push Notifications**: To alert you of chat messages, order status changes, and platform announcements.
- **Analytics & Improvements**: To monitor app performance, identify bugs, and improve the user experience.

---

## 3. Storage and Data Security

Your personal details and transaction logs are securely stored using **Google Firebase** services (Firestore, Auth, and Storage). We employ industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion of your personal data.

---

## 4. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- **Access & Correction**: You can view and edit your profile details at any time directly through the app's Account tab.
- **Data Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party integrations which have their own privacy policies:
- **Google Firebase**: For user authentication, database storage, file hosting, push notifications, and crash analytics.
- **Google Analytics**: For monitoring user behavior and application performance.
